Simply install the UndertaleWrapper-signed.apk on your phone!

For the controls, buy GameKeyboard+ from the Play Store and copy Undertale.gkp to your
phone. Install GameKeyboard+, click Settings, click Import profile and select the
Undertale.gkp.
Then, click on Macro Editor and change the first macro to the following:

Macro 1|67+13+20,0,0

Then go to the main app window and click start.

Open up Undertale and press the VolUp key! There you go :D
